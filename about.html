Our Story

From Lucknow to Across India

Task & Associates was founded with a simple belief: every business, regardless of size, deserves access to world-class consulting. What began as a small advisory practice in the heart of Lucknow has grown into a comprehensive consulting firm serving clients across diverse industries and geographies.

Our founding team of chartered accountants, company secretaries, and management professionals recognized a gap in the market — businesses in tier-2 cities often lacked access to the kind of strategic, multi-disciplinary advisory that large metros took for granted. We set out to change that.

Today, with a team of 15+ experts, we combine local market knowledge with global best practices to deliver solutions that are both practical and forward-thinking.

Meet Our Team

Meet the partners leading Task & Associates — blending deep technical expertise with real-world business and international experience.

CA. Ashish Asthana

Founder & Managing Partner

An FCA and ICAI member since 2000 with 25+ years' experience, Ashish specialises in Anti-Money Laundering (AML) and tax compliance across India and the UAE. He leads the firm's practice of ~2,000 clients and speaks regularly on corporate tax and regional financial strategy.

CA. Abhishek Asthana

Senior Partner

A Fellow Chartered Accountant (FCA) with 20+ years across consulting, academia and corporate leadership, Abhishek specialises in IFRS, litigation management and FINTECH. Formerly Associate Dean at NMIMS Mumbai and Associate Director at KPMG, he heads our Litigation Management and FINTECH verticals.

CA. Ritesh Srivastava

Senior Partner

A Chartered Accountant with 20+ years' post-qualification experience in audit, taxation and finance, Ritesh represents corporate, firm and individual clients in income-tax assessments and appeals up to the Tribunal. He also advises NRI and UAE clients on Indian tax compliance, with deep expertise in GST and VAT across India and the UAE.
